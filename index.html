Our story

Named for the drip that never rushes.

Moon Drop began as a single pour-over stand run out of a converted greenhouse, where the founders noticed that coffee, like the moon, has its own unhurried rhythm — rising slowly, changing shape through the day, never quite the same twice.

Every cup here is timed to that rhythm. Our baristas track slow-drip batches through the night so the first customer at dawn gets coffee that's been quietly becoming itself for twelve hours.

No rushing, no neon, no laptops-only policy. Just good light, better coffee, and a room built for lingering.
